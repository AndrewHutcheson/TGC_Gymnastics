21 April 2012 - Texas A&M Men Repeat as National Champs
The Texas A&M Men won the team title edging out Penn State at the annual NAIGC National Championships held in Salt Lake City, Utah 19-21 April. The meet featured more than 600 competitors from over 100 Universities and Colleges from around the country with both men’s and women’s teams competing simultaneously. The TGC was well represented with teams and individuals from Texas, Texas A&M, Texas State, Texas Tech, University of Texas Dallas, Hardin Simmons, and St. Mary’s.
In the men’s All Around, three-time defending national champion Eli Richardson was edged out by his teammate, Michael Bynum, from Texas A&M, but still won Floor. Meanwhile, newcomer Tommy Trumpeter from the University of Texas at Dallas won both Pommel Horse and Rings. Bryce Cashell, Texas State, claimed the national Vault title.
Next year’s NAIGC Nationals will be held in Minneapolis, MN.

31 March 2012 - Aggies Take TGC State Titles at the University of Texas

The Aggie Men and Women cruised to the TGC State Titles at the historic Gregory Gymnasium on the University of Texas Campus. The men’s All Around was a tie between A&M teammates Chase Hames and Will Conte (added this year from Connecticut). Chase Hames also won vault, but Tommy Trumpeter from this year’s newest team, the University of Texas at Dallas, won pommels, rings, and high bar. Aggie Alexis Olsen won the state title on floor, with Longhorn Mudeer Habeeb taking the parallel bars state title.
The women’s All Around was won by Sarah Thomas from A&M, going undefeated for the TGC season. The Aggies won 3 of 4 TGC State Titles with
Molly Stuckey taking floor, Michelle Owen winning beam, and Molly Stuckey with Sarah Thomas sharing the top spot on floor. The Longhorns Whitney Franklin had an impressive beam routine to win beam.
Due to TGC Rules, out-of-state teams were not eligible for TGC State Championship awards.


Aggie Men Win TGC State Meet

24 March 2012 - OU Men, Texas A&M Women Win at Texas State

The OU Men’s Club Team once again showed their dominance winning at Texas State University in San Marcos on 24 March in the Bobcats home gym. The Aggie Women continued their undefeated streak for the season taking the top spot. With Eli Richardson resting an injury, Josiah Thomas from OU claimed the men’s All-Around. Sarah Thomas from Texas A&M remained unbeaten in the TGC season in the women’s All-Around.

10 March 2012 - OU Men, Aggie Women Win at Texas A&M

The Oklahoma University Men’s Club Team rolled across the Red River into College Station to knock off the Aggie Men in their home gym by only 1.2 points. However, the Aggies took both the Men’s and Women’s All-Around titles with repeat wins for Eli Richardson (80.7) and Sarah Thomas (36.9).

2 March 2012 - Texas A&M Men Lose to China & US Olympic Training Center at HNI
The Aggie Men finished 11th to an International Field, including US World Championship Team Members Steve Legendre and Jonathon Horton, at the annual Houston National Invitational (HNI).

18 February 2012 - Aggie Men & Women Take First Meet at Texas Tech
After floods and other meet delays, the TGC finally kicked off the season in Lubbock, Texas with the Aggies taking both the Men’s and Women’s Team and All-Around titles. Eli Richardson has returned for another year to lead the Aggies with all-around, rings, and high bar firsts. A&M’s Sarah Thomas is also back, winning the all-around, bars, and beam.
