College Teams in Texas
Updated 4/4/13
Baylor University
The Baylor Bears train at Zero Gravity Gymnastics Club, located in Hewitt, three days a week. Members without cars normally have no problem working out carpools with other teammates. Practices for everyone (gymnasts & tumblers) are on Tuesday and Thursday from 8-10pm. We have special practices for experienced gymnasts only on Wednesdays from 7-9pm. Also, on some Fridays, we meet up around 4pm at the SLC or Bear Trail to do running or conditioning.
Baylor Gymnastics Club

Rice University

The Rice Gymnastics and Tumbling Club is a club for students of any ability who wish to practice women's or men's gymnastics, as well as cheerleading tumbling. We have several practices per week.

Texas A&M University
2007 Aggie Team

Open practice Monday through Thursday from 6 p.m. - 9 p.m.at Kyle Field in the 3rd floor of the Read building.
The Texas A&M Gymnastics Club is one of the oldest clubs in the history of the University. The club was formed by non-scholarship students who loved to tumble. In keeping with that tradition, we encourage all levels of Gymnasts, from Elite to first-timer, to learn and practice in a safe and friendly environment.
